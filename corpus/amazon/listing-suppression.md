## Common Suppression Reasons
Listings get suppressed or show errors for: missing main image or image policy
violations, restricted keywords in title, category approval missing, price alerts
(price far above/below expected), missing mandatory attributes, or brand
gating / IP complaints.

## Finding the Reason
Seller Central > Catalogue > "Fix your products" (or Manage Inventory >
suppressed filter) lists each suppressed SKU with the specific issue code.

## Fixing and Appealing
1. Open the SKU issue detail and follow the listed fix (image, attribute, etc.).
2. If the stated reason looks wrong, use "Contact us" from the issue page and
   include: SKU, ASIN, the error code, what you already fixed, and screenshots.
3. IP-complaint suppressions need either proof of authorisation (brand letter,
   invoices from authorised distributor) or removal of the infringing content.
4. Reinstatement timelines vary by issue type; track status under Case Log.
