## Payment Cycle
Meesho settles payments for delivered orders on a roughly 7-day cycle after
delivery [VERIFY your exact cycle in Supplier Panel > Payments]. An order
generally becomes payable only after its customer return window (7 days from
delivery) has passed. Detailed payment reports in the panel show earnings and
every deduction, including return adjustments, commission (Meesho charges
zero commission on most categories) and shipping charges.

## Why Payments Get Held or Deducted
Common reasons: bank account verification pending, GSTIN/KYC name mismatch
with the bank account, high return-rate or quality investigation on the
account, or recoveries/adjustments against approved return claims and
penalties. The reason typically appears against the settlement entry in the
Payments section [VERIFY].

## Resolving a Hold
1. Open Supplier Panel > Payments and read the stated reason on the held
   settlement.
2. For KYC or bank issues, re-verify details under Settings (bank account,
   GSTIN). Name mismatches between GST registration and bank account are the
   most common cause - keep GST certificate and a cancelled cheque ready.
3. If no reason is shown, or documents were already submitted, raise a
   Support ticket quoting the settlement ID, affected order IDs, and
   screenshots of the hold.
4. Track the ticket from the Support section until the settlement releases.
